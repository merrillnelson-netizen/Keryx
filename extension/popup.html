How It Works

1. Open messages.google.com in this browser.
2. Open a conversation — new messages are automatically intercepted.
3. Each message is deduped and sent to your Keryx relay, which stores it and runs AI analysis.
4. Fan-out destinations (OGBilliards Pro, etc.) receive a copy too.
